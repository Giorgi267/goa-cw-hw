Simple Omelette Recipe

A savory and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter in a frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour the eggs into the pan and let them cook gently.
  4. Add any fillings and fold the omelette.
  5. Serve immediately.
